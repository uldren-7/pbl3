Proposed Methodology

This project approaches the problem by modeling green space placement as a constraint satisfaction problem (CSP). The goal is to determine the optimal locations for green spaces such that the cooling effect is maximized while satisfying real-world constraints.

System Workflow

  • Collect or simulate urban spatial and temperature data
  • Identify areas with high heat concentration (UHI hotspots)
  • Define constraints such as land availability and budget limits
  • Apply optimization techniques to determine ideal placement
  • Generate a final plan for green space distribution

Key Idea

Based on insights from recent research, including Lin & Li (2025), the effectiveness of green spaces depends not only on their type but also significantly on their spatial configuration. Therefore, this project prioritizes placement optimization over simply increasing green coverage.
