Data Sources

The project relies on a combination of real-world and simulated data to model urban conditions and evaluate the effectiveness of green space placement.

  • Urban temperature data to identify heat distribution patterns
  • Land use maps to determine available and restricted areas
  • Energy consumption data to estimate cooling demand

Data Challenges

High-resolution urban datasets are often not publicly available. To address this limitation, the project may use simulated data based on realistic urban patterns and assumptions.

Assumptions

  • The cooling effect of green spaces is influenced by their spatial placement
  • Urban temperature patterns can be approximated using available data
  • Energy consumption is correlated with temperature levels
