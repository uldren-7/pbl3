Expected Outcomes

The project aims to generate an optimized plan for placing green spaces in urban environments to achieve maximum cooling efficiency.

  • Identification of high-impact locations for green space placement
  • Reduction in average urban temperature in targeted areas
  • Decrease in electricity consumption due to reduced cooling needs

Impact

By strategically placing green spaces, the system can improve urban comfort, reduce energy costs, and contribute to environmental sustainability. The results can also support city planners in making informed decisions.

Future Possibilities

The model can be extended with real-time data integration and applied to different cities for large-scale urban planning.
